Intro

My name is GX.
I obtained BA in Computer Science and Government from Wesleyan, and am currently a MSCS student at UCLA.
I work on LMs and other deep generative models, DALLE/GLIDE/SSM;
Multi-modality is my current focus, aiming to use language-vision pretraining to create infrastructures and tools for game and animation making.
Meanwhile, fairness and equality in AI also intrigues me, aiming to build AIs that reduces social inequalities and biases.
